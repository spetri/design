W E L C O M E

I'm Sabba, a front end developer passionate about product mgmt and code.

And I want to work for you.

I graduated with two degrees: Computer Science and Art History.

After university, I took my Computer Science degree and spent a few years as a full-stack developer in an Agile environment. I loved working on a team to solve challenging technical problems, as well as take part in the development and evolution of our cultural processes. What started as a mishmash of segmented teams without process slowly evolved into one unified team, learning to perform stand-ups, pair programming, estimation practices, and much more, all under an Agile umbrella.

Over time, my vision and scope began to broaden, as I enjoyed looking at product vision and how they could be enhanced from a market viewpoint. I also began to take strong interest in the other teams working with the developers and formed stronger bonds between them. Beginning to wield my Art History background, I analyzed and interpreted user workflows, consulted with the business to elicit requirements / discussed user experience, and took a fresh look at the business processes that surrounded our products.

Coming back to my California home, I've taken the knowledge gained and put it to good use. While I will always write code and continue to contribute to open-source projects, my focus is now toward product management and design.
